Paul Browne

Born and raised in one of Dublin's most musical families, Paul has been emersed in traditional Irish music since he was a child, learning many songs and instruments, proficient in guitar, violin, bodhrán, banjo and more, concentrating primarily on playing guitar.

Although very much a part of the traditional Irish music scene, flavours of jazz and classical can be heard throughout Paul's rhythmic style of guitar playing and fiery fiddle forms.

A multi-instrumentalist, Paul is an accomplished fiddle player, along with adept rythmic bodhrán and rich bouzouki accompaniment, Paul has many strings to his bow!

Having studied music on an academic level combined with decades of live music performances, Paul is one of Ireland's most saught after guitar players for traditional Irish music, appearing on many recordings, and performing in some of Ireland's finest venues and major venues throughout the world. Paul is the winner of the "Best Musician" award at the Agrigento International Folk Festival. He has performed with Ireland's finest traditional musicians, including Peter Browne, Máire Breathnach and Niamh Parsons. An in demand rhythm guitarist for Irish dancers Paul is the resident guitarsit on the Celtic Nights show at the Arlington Hotel Dublin. Paul is also a source of song in the Irish language, collecting some rare gems.

Finally, after all these years Paul is set to release his first solo album - The Journey.
