Paul Browne

Born and raised in one of Dublin's most musical families, Paul has been emersed in traditional Irish music since he was a child, learning many songs and instruments, proficient in guitar, violin, bodhran, banjo and more, concentrating primarily on playing guitar.

Although very much a part of the traditional Irish music scene, influences of jazz and classical can be heard throughout Paul's rhythmic style of guitar playing.

Having studied music on an academic level combined with decades of live music performances, Paul is one of Ireland's most saught after guitar players for traditional Irish music, appearing on many recordings.

Finally, after all these years Paul is set to release his first solo album - The Journey.
